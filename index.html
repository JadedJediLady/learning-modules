Protein-Protein Docking

Participants will use computational modeling to assess antibody-protein binding in the context of a controlled challenge leveraging online docking servers, CSM-AB and AlphaFold3. Some coding experience is encouraged. Participation is open to students, post docs, early career professionals and clinicians.
